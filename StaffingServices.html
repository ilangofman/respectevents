What Food do We Serve?


The serving team has a wide exposure in banquets of different cultures, and styles. This includes French Service, buffet service, Russian service, plate service and many more.

We are also familiar with multicultural cuisine including Antipasto Bar of European functions.

Our banquet services have a strict restriction of Kosher events.

Our team has done a lot of different complications and traditions as a late night seafood buffet of Portuguese function, and unique buffet stations in Indian banquets.

If there are any questions, please don't hesitate to ask.


What is included in our Staffing Service



    Servers and Wait-Staff

  • Carries out full set-up of function space including tableware, seating arrangements and display decor
  • Conducts all table service in a timely and pleasant manner
  • Responsible for tear down and clean up of function space
